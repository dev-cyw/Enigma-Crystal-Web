About

Enigma Crystal is a challenging ROM hack that revitalizes the Johto experience with enhanced level scaling and quality of life improvements. This hack introduces a plethora of changes, such as addition of newer Pokémon up to Generation 9, and various items such as the Infinite Candy, a Portable PC, and a Restore Kit. The encounters have been completely overhauled and improved, utilizing the HG-Engine extensively to achieve this. Discover back-to-back trainer fights, expanded storage boxes, and various visual enhancements in this revamped Johto journey!
