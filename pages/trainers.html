Trainers

In this game there are various types of trainer battles and areas
Boss Fight -> A Important/Harder Trainer
Gauntlet -> Same team for all fights on current route/area
Back to Back -> Next 2 Trainers are directly after eachother no changing party or healing
Optional -> A Trainer that is not needed to progress but gives a reward for beating them
